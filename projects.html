CLAYTON TUTTY CONSTRUCTION

BATHROOM ADDITION IN POINT CHEVALIER

Work completed for Clayton Tutty Construction

Clayton Tutty’s clients wanted to extend their home and opted for a second-storey addition, which required new plumbing to be connected to their existing plumbing.

The team at Clayton Tutty Construction are fantastic to work with. We came into the project with a good understanding of what the builders and client wanted and took direction from both throughout the build.

The architect presented us with clear plans about what was required. Working through the plans to connect into the existing plumbing was straightforward.

PLUMBING WORK REQUIRED:

  • New bathroom
  • Connecting new plumbing on the house’s second storey to existing plumbing on the first storey
  • Installation of an instantaneous water heater
